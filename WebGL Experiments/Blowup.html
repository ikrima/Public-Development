Clicking on the image will blow up the video into little pieces. Technical notes:

  • Using canvas, double buffering, and js to render. I imagine the drawimage calls get translated to gl calls
  • Using ogg codec; brings firefox to its knees
  • Works well in Chrome/IE9
  • Pretty good perf "out of box"; no need for shaders yet. Res is 640x360, 780 kbps video, 171kbps audio, 24 fps
  • Work/code based on Sean Christmann's demo.
